Scrambled Eggs Done Right

Description

The right way to scramble eggs. There is more to just mixing eggs and cooking! This will make a believer out of you.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. mayonaise
  • 1 tsp. water (optional)
  • 1 tsp. maragrine or butter
  • 1 pinch salt and peppter to taste

Steps

  1. Add mayonaise, water, and egg onto bowl
  2. Whisk together the eggs using a fork
  3. Melt margargine or butter into pan over low heat
  4. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook
  5. Plate when eggs ar firm, but moist.
  6. Add salt & pepper as needed.
